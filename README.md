# ShiftHire

**AI-powered candidate sourcing that turns a job description into a ranked LinkedIn shortlist in under 5 minutes.**

ShiftHire automates the full recruitment sourcing pipeline — from reading your JD and generating precision search queries, to scanning live LinkedIn profiles, evaluating each candidate, and returning a scored, ranked shortlist with written reasoning. No LinkedIn Recruiter licence required.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Matching** — reads your job description end-to-end and constructs semantic search queries that capture nuanced requirements beyond simple keyword matching.
- **LinkedIn Deep Search** — scans millions of live LinkedIn profiles using semantic matching, surfacing candidates who genuinely fit the role.
- **Automated Candidate Evaluation** — every candidate is scored 0–100, assigned a match band (Strong / Good / Below), and given bullet-point reasoning. No black box.
- **Real-Time Progress Tracking** — a live processing screen shows you exactly where your search is at while it runs.
- **Up to 50 Candidates per Search** — configurable from 5 to 50 candidates per job match.
- **CSV Export** — one-click export of your full ranked shortlist including names, LinkedIn URLs, match scores, and AI reasoning — ready for your ATS.
- **Credit-Based Billing** — pay only for what you use. Free tier included, no card required to start.
- **GDPR Compliant** — no candidate data stored beyond your session, no third-party data sharing.

## How It Works

1. **Create an account & sign in** — 100 free credits included, no card required.
2. **Click "New Job"** — opens a fresh job match form in your dashboard.
3. **Paste your job description** — include role, required skills, seniority, and any must-haves. Skip company name and branding.
4. **Set the location** — use `City, Country` format (e.g. `London, UK` or `Dubai, UAE`).
5. **Choose an experience level** — Entry / Mid / Senior / Lead.
6. **Submit** — ShiftHire runs the full pipeline automatically. Your shortlist is ready in 2–3 minutes.

## Usage Guide

### Creating a Job Match

1. Sign in to your dashboard.
2. Click **New Job Match**.
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Job Description** (required) — paste the full JD. Omit company name and branding for best results.
   - **Location** — `City, Country` format. Examples: `Toronto, Canada` · `Singapore` · `Remote — APAC`.
   - **Experience Level** — Entry / Mid / Senior / Lead (recommended).
   - **Number of Candidates** — use the slider to select 5–50 (default: 20).
4. Click **Find Matching Candidates**.

### Reading the Results

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| Rank | Overall position (1 = best match) |
| Name | Links directly to their LinkedIn profile |
| Title | Their current job title |
| Location | Where they are based |
| Match % | 0–100 AI match score |
| Band | Strong (70+) / Good (50–70) / Below (under 50) |

Click **Details** on any row to see the full AI reasoning behind the match.

### Filtering & Sorting

- Set a **Min match %** to filter out lower-scoring candidates.
- Click **Match %** or **Rank** column headers to re-sort.
- Filter by job status on the dashboard: All / Pending / Processing / Completed / Failed.

### Exporting

Click **Export CSV** to download: Rank, Name, LinkedIn URL, Title, Location, Match Score, Band, and AI reasoning for every candidate.
